What is Zupp?


1. Zupp is a technology enabled social platform that enables users, called “Travellers”, who are driving their car or any other vehicle from point A to point B to offer rides to one or more users, called “Co-travellers”, travelling in the same direction who are willing to ride along with the “Traveller”. The term “Traveller” in the context of Zupp refers to the user who is offering seats in his or her vehicle and he or she may or may not be the actual Owner of the vehicle. The terms “Traveller” and “Co-traveller” as also used purely in the scope of a single carpool transaction in Zupp.
2. The incentive for the Traveller and Co-traveller to carpool or share rides might be to commute using a sustainable and eco-friendly manner or to save on fuel cost or to cut costs on more expensive/tedious commuting options or to reduce traffic on the roads or to reduce/avoid driving stress.
3. Zupp provides capabilities to enterprises to offer/promote this platform to their employees as a sustainable, safe and economical commute option, free of charge.
4. Zupp does not own or operate cars or any vehicle for transporting its users. Zupp does not have any contract with its “Trevellers” or “Co-travellers” or provide guarantee/assurance that its “Travellers” will discover “Co-travellers” or vice versa.
5. Zupp is currently a product that is free for use for both “Traveller” and for “Co-traveller”. However, we reserve the right to charge for the service at any point of time and will provide prior notice to all existing users in such an event by way of a notice on the website and also by changing these Terms of Use appropriately on our application.

How do I use Zupp?


Zupp allows registered users to opt to be a “Traveller” and offer rides during inter and intra-city trips or become a "Co-traveller" by searching for prospective rides on through Zupp
- The "Traveller" can specify the ride details by selecting a valid starting point, ending point, route and time of departure. Other mandatory data points include Car details like number plate details, colour, AC/Non-AC, etc.
- The "Co-treveller" also needs to give his/her starting point and destination for travel
- Basis Zupp's patented "route-matching" algorithm, a traveller is shown options of co-travellers travelling on the same route and vice-versa. Prospective travellers will be shown to a co-traveller basis his/her route match with the other user -displayed in terms of percentage of match (Higher the route match, higher the ranking)
- Zupp also prioritizes search results basis ratings of every user (which is based on the feedback of experience provided by other fellow users who have carpooled with this user before). Ratings used to determine rankings is purely based on Zupp algorithm which takes into account previous user feedback, route matching percentage, and also few other details. Zupp in no manner is liable in case of any user discomfort caused due to our rating system. Users are free to choose and avail carpool with other users of any rating.
- Once a traveller or a co-traveller selects a particular user to carpool with, he/she sends a confirmation to the other user. When the other user also confirms (shows willingness) to carpool with the first user (through the application) only then does the Zupp app send phone numbers of both users to the other user. Once the phone numbers are availed of to the users, the users are expected to contact each other and confirm the carpooling details such as pick-up and drop points.
- Basis the route match and the length of the route, Zupp also suggests a measure of how much saving/contribution the traveller would need to make if the carpool were to happen (In INR). If and only if the co-traveller is willing to pay the amount mentioned, to the traveller, should the co-traveller agree to carpool with the traveller through the confirm option.
- The benefits accrued by the traveller are retained only by the traveller, in order for him/her to cut down on his cost of hosting the ride. The traveller does not owe any percentage of this amount to the Zupp app. Zupp is merely a platform connecting different users basis their travelling requirements.
- Zupp also allows users to invite their friends for using the app, completely free of charge.
- All users can see a history of their rides in the application panel and provide genuine feedback on their ride experiences through Zupp. These feedbacks and ratings would be visible to other users subsequently.
- Users agree and accept that all of the information provided to Zupp while setting up their user accounts and at any other time shall be true, correct, complete and accurate in all respects. Members also agree that any information supplied to Zupp or posted on the Site in connection with any Trip, Vehicle or Car Sharing will be true, accurate and complete.


What about Use Safety?


You are responsible for your safety while carpooling on Zupp. Zupp is only a technology enabled social platform which helps you search for, setup and complete carpools in order to use sustainable means of commuting, reduce fuel cost, reduce stress associated with driving and reduce overall traffic on the roads. You are encouraged to use Zupp responsibly and safely. Zupp recommends the following safety guidelines in the course of using our product.
1. Zupp validates mobile number during user registration by sending an automated code to your phone number. Zupp does not undertake any other user checks at an individual level.
2. Users are expected to monitor the 'Profile completion status' of prospective user before selecting to carpool with them. Profile completion status indicates the following:
- Phone number verification
- Official email verification
- Govt. ID verification
3. While Zupp encourages users to provide official government ID upload, Zupp in no manner takes the responsibility of verifying the authenticity of that ID. Zupp merely ensures that the user's name provided on facebook profile and the government ID are a match.
4. Users are advised to carry a photo ID/company ID and show the same at the pickup point while take you take a ride and request the same from your ride partner.
5. If you are a co-traveller, choose a safe pick up location which will not compromise your safety when you are waiting for the Traveller to arrive.
6. If you are a Traveller choose a pick up location which does not compromise your safety on the road or disrupt traffic if you have to wait for the Passenger to arrive.
7. As a traveller, do not take in more co-travellers than the recommended seating capacity of your vehicle.
8. Use of mobile phones while driving is extremely dangerous. Do not under any circumstances use your mobile phone either to coordinate a carpool using Zupp or for any other reason.
9. Zupp recommends using the product to set up carpools before you start driving or to park your car in safe designated parking areas if you have to use the product or coordinate with your co-travellers on the way.
10. The Phone number of the other person with whom your ride match is done will be shown to you. Please use discretion in using it.
11. Exercise overall caution and common sense while using Zupp.
